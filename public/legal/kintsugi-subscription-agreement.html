$KINTSUGI TOKEN SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

A Product Token of b0ase.com
Agreement Version 1.0 — January 2026

BETWEEN:

(1) b0ase Ventures ("the Company"), operator of b0ase.com

AND

(2) The Subscriber (details in Schedule 1)

1. BACKGROUND

1.1 The Company has developed "Kintsugi Engine" — an artificial intelligence system that automates the creation of startup infrastructure including token generation, website creation, social media setup, and KYC integration.

1.2 The Company has created $KINTSUGI tokens to represent fractional ownership and revenue rights in the Kintsugi Engine product line.

1.3 The Subscriber wishes to purchase $KINTSUGI tokens on the terms set out in this Agreement.

2. DEFINITIONS

"$KINTSUGI Tokens" means the digital tokens representing ownership interest in the Kintsugi Engine product, with a total supply of 1,000,000,000 (one billion) tokens.

"Subscription Amount" means the amount in GBP set out in Schedule 1.

"Token Allocation" means the number of $KINTSUGI tokens allocated to the Subscriber as set out in Schedule 1.

"Kintsugi Engine" means the AI-powered startup creation platform operated at b0ase.com/kintsugi or such other URL as the Company may designate.

3. SUBSCRIPTION

3.1 Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Subscriber agrees to subscribe for the Token Allocation in consideration for the Subscription Amount.

3.2 The Subscription Amount shall be paid within 14 days of signing this Agreement via:

  • Bank transfer to the Company's designated account; or
  • BSV (Bitcoin SV) to the Company's designated wallet address; or
  • Such other payment method as agreed by the parties.

3.3 Upon receipt of the Subscription Amount, the Company shall allocate the Token Allocation to the Subscriber's account on the b0ase.com platform.

4. TOKEN RIGHTS

4.1 $KINTSUGI token holders are entitled to:

  • Revenue Share: Pro-rata share of 80% of net revenue generated by the Kintsugi Engine, distributed quarterly.
  • Governance: Voting rights on material product decisions, with votes weighted by token holdings.
  • Information: Quarterly reports on Kintsugi Engine performance, revenue, and development roadmap.
  • Transferability: Right to transfer tokens subject to Section 5.

4.2 $KINTSUGI tokens do not represent:

  • Equity or shares in b0ase Ventures or any legal entity;
  • Any claim on assets of the Company other than as specified herein;
  • Any guaranteed return or income.

5. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

5.1 Tokens may be transferred to third parties provided that:

  • The transferee completes KYC verification on the b0ase.com platform;
  • The transfer is recorded on the b0ase.com token registry; and
  • The Company is notified of the transfer within 7 days.

5.2 The Company has no right of first refusal on token transfers.

6. REVENUE DISTRIBUTION

6.1 Revenue generated by Kintsugi Engine shall be distributed as follows:

Recipient Share Notes
$KINTSUGI Token Holders 80% Distributed pro-rata quarterly
b0ase Operations 20% Platform maintenance, development

6.2 Distributions shall be made in GBP, BSV, or stablecoin at the Company's discretion, within 30 days of quarter end.

7. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

7.1 Subscriber Representations

The Subscriber represents and warrants that:

  • They have the legal capacity to enter into this Agreement;
  • The Subscription Amount represents funds lawfully obtained;
  • They understand the risks associated with digital tokens;
  • They are not subscribing with funds they cannot afford to lose;
  • They have conducted their own due diligence on the Kintsugi Engine.

7.2 Company Representations

The Company represents and warrants that:

  • It has the right to issue $KINTSUGI tokens;
  • The Kintsugi Engine is operational or in active development;
  • It will use Subscription Amounts primarily for Kintsugi Engine development and operations;
  • It will provide quarterly reports to token holders.

8. RISK FACTORS

The Subscriber acknowledges the following risks:

  • Technology Risk: AI systems may not perform as expected.
  • Market Risk: Demand for startup automation services may fluctuate.
  • Regulatory Risk: Token regulations may change.
  • Competition Risk: Competitors may develop similar solutions.
  • Liquidity Risk: There may be no secondary market for tokens.
  • Total Loss: The Subscriber may lose the entire Subscription Amount.

9. TERMINATION

9.1 This Agreement may be terminated by mutual written consent of the parties.

9.2 If the Kintsugi Engine ceases operations permanently, the Company shall distribute any remaining treasury to token holders on a pro-rata basis.

10. GOVERNING LAW

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.

10.2 Any disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under the LCIA Rules.
