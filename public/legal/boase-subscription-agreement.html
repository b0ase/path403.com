1. BACKGROUND

1.1 b0ase Ventures operates b0ase.com, a venture studio that develops and launches technology products including AI systems, blockchain applications, and developer tools.

1.2 The Studio has created $BOASE tokens to represent fractional ownership and revenue rights in the entire b0ase portfolio of products.

1.3 The Studio maintains a 50% ownership stake in each product it creates, including but not limited to: Kintsugi Engine ($KINTSUGI), ScrollPay ($SCROLLPAY), and all future products.

1.4 The Subscriber wishes to purchase $BOASE tokens on the terms set out in this Agreement.

5. TOKEN RIGHTS

5.1 $BOASE token holders are entitled to:

  • Portfolio Revenue Share: Pro-rata share of revenue flowing to the Studio from its 50% stakes in all products, distributed quarterly.
  • Studio Governance: Voting rights on studio-level decisions including new product launches, major investments, and strategic direction.
  • Information Rights: Quarterly reports on all portfolio products, studio financials, and development roadmaps.
  • Transferability: Right to transfer tokens subject to Section 6.
  • On-Chain Ownership: $BOASE tokens exist on the BSV blockchain and can be held in compatible wallets.

5.2 $BOASE tokens do not represent:

  • Equity or shares in any incorporated legal entity;
  • Direct ownership in any individual product (those are represented by Product Tokens);
  • Any claim on assets of the Studio other than as specified herein;
  • Any guaranteed return or income.

6. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

6.1 Tokens may be transferred to third parties provided that:

  • The transferee completes KYC verification on the b0ase.com platform (for platform transfers); or
  • The transfer occurs on-chain via the BSV blockchain (for wallet-to-wallet transfers);
  • The Studio is notified of significant transfers (>1% of supply) within 7 days.

6.2 The Studio has no right of first refusal on token transfers.

7. REVENUE DISTRIBUTION

7.1 Revenue flows to $BOASE holders through the following mechanism:

Source Flow
Product Revenue (e.g., Kintsugi) 80% to Product Token Holders (incl. Studio's 50%)
Studio's Product Share (50% of 80%) 40% of each product's revenue flows to Studio
Studio Revenue to $BOASE Holders 80% distributed pro-rata to $BOASE holders
Studio Operations 20% retained for operations & development

7.2 Distributions shall be made in GBP, BSV, stablecoin, or crypto at the Studio's discretion, within 30 days of quarter end.
