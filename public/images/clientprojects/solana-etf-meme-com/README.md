## Project Overview

Solana ETF Meme is a satirical yet functional decentralized finance platform that gamifies cryptocurrency trading through meme culture and community-driven investment strategies. The platform combines the viral nature of meme coins with the serious infrastructure of exchange-traded funds (ETFs), creating a unique hybrid approach to decentralized investing.
