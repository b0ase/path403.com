## Project Overview

Tribify AI is a revolutionary community-building platform that uses artificial intelligence to create, manage, and optimize online tribes and communities. The platform leverages advanced AI algorithms to enhance community engagement, automate moderation, and facilitate meaningful connections between members.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Community Creation**: Automated community setup and optimization
- **Intelligent Moderation**: AI-driven content moderation and community management
- **Smart Matching**: AI algorithm for connecting like-minded community members
- **Engagement Analytics**: Deep insights into community health and member behavior
- **Automated Content Curation**: AI-powered content recommendation and organization
- **Token-Based Governance**: $TRIBE token integration for community decision-making
