## Project Overview

Vex Void is an ambient trip-hop, dark underground musician and AV artist. Fusing experimental electronic music, live visuals, and cyberpunk aesthetics, Vex Void creates immersive audio-visual experiences for underground music and art lovers.

## Key Features
- Ambient trip-hop and experimental electronic music
- Live AV performances and immersive visual shows
- Cyberpunk and dark underground aesthetic
- NFT and token-enabled music/art releases

## Technology & Artistry
- **Music Production:** Hardware synths, samplers, modular rigs, and digital audio workstations
- **Visuals:** Real-time generative art, projection mapping, and custom VJ software
- **Web:** Next.js, React, Three.js, WebGL for interactive web experiences
