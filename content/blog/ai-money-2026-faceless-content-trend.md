## Summary

A significant trend is emerging in the AI-info industry for 2026: **faceless content creation powered by AI**. This represents the "next bull run" for information products.

But theory is cheap. We're going to show you exactly how it works with a real project: **Zero Dice**, an AI-powered entertainment brand generating comics, stories, music, and ASMR content—all without a human face in sight.

## Key Points

**The AI Normalization**

- AI will achieve "normie knowledge" status in 2026 (mainstream awareness)
- Info entrepreneurs have been capitalizing on AI for 1-2 years, teaching:
  - How to make money with AI
  - How to create courses
  - How to monetize knowledge

**The Core Opportunity: Solving the Face Fear**

The Paradox:
- Showing your face online (TikTok, etc.) can make you richer than traditional jobs in 6 months
- Yet people are terrified of judgment and won't do it
- We live in an era where faceless content can generate income, but fear holds people back

The Solution Info Marketers Found: Small accounts are emerging across platforms, making money WITHOUT showing faces:
- Automated YouTube channels
- Instagram theme pages
- X/Twitter faceless brands

**Why This Trend Will Explode**

1. **High demand**: "How to make money" always sells best among the big three (money/status, health, relationships)
2. **Low barrier**: Removes the biggest friction point (fear of judgment)
3. **AI enablement**: Tools now exist to create content without personal appearance
4. **Proven model**: Early adopters are already monetizing successfully

## The Secret Weapon: Clipping Campaigns

These faceless accounts didn't build audiences slowly - they appeared from nowhere and went viral in weeks.

**The Strategy:** Coordinated mass distribution of organic short-form content - "clipping campaigns"

- Beat the algorithms by incentivizing armies of thousands to repurpose your content
- Pay them when they go viral
- Turn yourself into a celebrity overnight

**The Problem:** You still need to show your face to become one of the biggest known people on platforms. 90% of the audience is scared to post even when only their mom and best friend will see it.

## The Eureka Moment: AI Video Generation

AI video generation became sophisticated enough to solve the final piece:

**The Old Requirements:**
1. Good knowledge about info products
2. Understanding what sells
3. Strong personal branding
4. Either be a cold traffic pro with ad budget OR expose yourself through organic clipping

**The New Reality:**
- Remove the process of creating brands
- Remove the process of creating offers
- Teach the ONLY thing that matters: **how to use AI to leverage attention**
