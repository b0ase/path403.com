## The Technical Architecture

Built with Three.js and WebGL, the Metanet renders 30+ projects in real-time 3D space with:

- **Fibonacci sphere distribution** - Projects are positioned using the golden ratio for optimal visual spacing
- **Unreal Bloom post-processing** - That neon glow isn't CSSâ€”it's actual light emission in 3D space
- **Custom GLSL shaders** - Connections pulse with morphing purple-to-red gradients
- **CSS2D labels** - Interactive pill-style labels float in 3D space but render as crisp HTML

The result is something that feels alive. Projects pulse. Connections flow. The camera slowly orbits around the ecosystem like you're watching a galaxy form.

## Why Visualize a Portfolio This Way?

Traditional portfolio pages are lists. Grids. Cards. They show you projects one at a time, in isolation.

But that's not how an ecosystem works.

Projects in the b0ase universe share tokens. They share users. Bitcoin Spreadsheets and Bitcoin Writer share the same authentication. FLOOP and Tribes Wallet share the same token infrastructure. Miss Void and Ninja Punk Girls share the same e-commerce engine.

The Metanet makes these connections visible. You can literally see which projects cluster together. You can see how live projects (green) anchor the network while beta projects (yellow) push outward into new territory.

## Interacting with the Metanet

**Orbit:** Click and drag to rotate the camera around the ecosystem

**Zoom:** Scroll to zoom in and out (20-100 unit range)

**Hover:** Float over any project pill to see it highlighted
